Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I get started?
A: Simply open the app, choose a coach personality, and start chatting. You can optionally add context about your goals and projects for more personalized coaching.
Q: Which AI powers Simon Says?
A: We use Google Gemini 2.5 Flash, the latest multimodal AI model capable of understanding text, voice, and images.
Q: Is my data private?
A: Yes. Your conversations are stored securely in Firebase Firestore with authentication-based access controls. Only you can access your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.
Q: How does the Coach Marketplace work?
A: Creators can publish specialized AI coaches for $2.99-$19.99. Buyers make one-time purchases for lifetime access. Creators receive 70% of revenue.
Q: Can I create my own custom coach?
A: Yes! Use the custom coach creator to design coaches with specific expertise, personality, and communication style. You can keep them private or publish to the marketplace.
Q: How do voice conversations work?
A: On web, we use the Web Speech API for voice input and output. Speak naturally and the AI will respond with voice. (Note: Voice features are browser-only currently.)
Q: Can I upload images?
A: Yes! Gemini 2.5 Flash supports multimodal input. Upload images and the AI can analyze and provide context-aware coaching based on visual information.
Q: Is there a free version?
A: Yes! The core app with 6 built-in coach personalities is completely free. Only marketplace coaches require payment.
Q: How do refunds work?
A: Marketplace purchases are generally non-refundable. We provide refunds only for technical issues preventing access, duplicate purchases, or fraud. Request within 7 days.
Q: Can I delete my account?
A: Yes. Contact us at support@simonsayscoach.com to request account deletion. All your data will be permanently removed.

Troubleshooting

Issue: AI responses are slow
Solution: Check your internet connection. Gemini API typically responds in 2-5 seconds. If consistently slow, contact support.
Issue: Voice not working
Solution: Voice features require browser permissions and are web-only currently. Check that microphone permissions are granted.
Issue: Can't see purchased coach
Solution: Ensure you're logged into the same account. Check your custom coaches list. If still missing, contact support with purchase details.
Issue: Marketplace not loading
Solution: Check internet connection and try refreshing. If Firebase is down, marketplace may be temporarily unavailable.
